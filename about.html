About

Hi! I am Marta Pey, a passionate artist, illustrator and graphic designer based in Spain, with a deep love for creativity in all its forms.

Digital drawing has become one of my greatest passions, as well as creating stories, 3D modeling and animation, UX and UI Design, and a long list of creative ways to design my own space and imagine new ways of living, which up to now all it has given me has been pure joy.

In this portfolio I invite you to explore a sample of my work and to enjoy the journey through my creative world!
