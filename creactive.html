UX & UI Design

This university project consisted in the creation of an app based on UX & UI Design. Our idea was to integrate Daily Challenges related to photography, drawing, writing, video and audio, which involved creating interfaces and interactions that allowed users to complete these challenges effortessly.

My role in this project involved conceptualizing and designing the User Interface of the app, creating layouts, visual elements, and interactive components, ensuring it was intuitive, visually appealing and conducive.

In addition to UX design, I focused on optimizing the overall user experience throughout the app, which entailed understanding user behaviors, preferences and needs.
